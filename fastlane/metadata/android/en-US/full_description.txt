Triples is a beautiful, fast-paced card game inspired by the classic Set. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a complete beginner, Triples offers a variety of modes and features to provide a challenging and rewarding experience.

The rules are simple: find sets of three cards (Triples) where each property—number, shape, pattern, and color—is either the same on all three cards or different on all three.

Key Features:
- Classic Mode: Race against the clock to clear the entire deck.
- Arcade Mode: Find as many Triples as possible in 60 seconds.
- Zen Mode: A relaxed, infinite mode with no timers or pressure.
- Beginner Mode: Perfect for learning, featuring simplified cards with fewer properties.
- Modern UI: Fully updated with Material 3 design and full Dark Mode support.
- Customization: Personalize your game with adjustable card colors, shapes, and patterns.
- Smart Gameplay: Use Hints when you're stuck, or Shuffle the cards in play for a fresh perspective.
- Detailed Statistics: Track your progress with game timelines, round-specific records, and CSV export.
- Google Play Games: Sync your progress with Cloud Saves and compete on Leaderboards and Achievements.
